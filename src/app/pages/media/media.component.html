info How Media Servers Work

Media servers are endpoints that can host your content following the Nostr BUD (Blossom - Blobs stored simply on mediaservers) protocol.

Your primary server (the first in the list) is used by default for uploading new media. You can reorder servers by dragging and dropping them to change your primary server.

Media servers store your images, videos, and files, making them accessible to other Nostr users.
