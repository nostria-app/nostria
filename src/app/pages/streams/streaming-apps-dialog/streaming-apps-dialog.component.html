Publish your live stream from Nostria

Premium subscribers can push camera and microphone directly to OpenResist from the browser, verify the feed privately, and then publish the Nostr live stream event when ready.

If your provider only gives you a public watch page, Nostria can still publish the live stream event and link viewers there. Add a direct HLS or LiveKit playback URL only when you want inline playback inside Nostria.
