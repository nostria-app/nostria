Nostria uses advanced AI models that run directly on your device. This means:

  • Privacy First: Your data never leaves your device. No cloud servers are involved.
  • Offline Capable: Once downloaded, models work without an internet connection.
  • Local Processing: Processing happens on your hardware, which may be slower than cloud services depending on your device's capabilities.

Note: The first time you use an AI feature, the app will download and cache the necessary model. This may take a few moments and use some data.

Local image generation on the AI page uses the Janus Pro browser model and currently requires WebGPU support for practical performance.

Disable all AI features

Check this if you prefer not to use any AI features or want to save resources.
