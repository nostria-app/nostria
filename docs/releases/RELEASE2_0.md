All development happens in the open for Nostria, so if you've already been using it, you will already have experienced some of these new features.

# What's New in Nostria 2.0
## Music [/music](https://nostria.app/music)
Nostria 2.0 introduces native support for music sharing and discovery. Users can now upload, share, and listen to music tracks directly within the platform. This feature includes playlist creation and track uploading. Earn zaps and give appreciation to your favorite artists directly through zapping. When you publish music track or playlists, you can choose a custom set of relays, ensuring your music is available where you want it to be. We've also improved the music player, with tree different views, including one that mimics WinAmp with a functional equalizer. You can also view song lyrics.

## Publish Media
There is a new option to publish media content. This is a simpler way to share images and videos. When you publish media, your content is then optimized for display in apps that support media events. Nostria will automatically post a short form post with a preview of the media, making it discoverable across all types of Nostr clients. You find it when you click the "+" button to create a new post. You can quickly capture a photo with your phone and publish in seconds.

![nostria-publish-media.png](https://mibo.eu.nostria.app/6ed210450c9239988b101b0a287889ae8acb0798f01a765cc62e79c273f20e02.webp)

## Remote Signer
Improved support for remote signer through client-initiated connection. This allows users to connect to their remote signer by simply scanning a QR code, without needing to manually enter connection details.

## Content Reveal
You can now choose "Always reveal this user" on media, allowing you to automatically see content from specific users, without needing to follow them.

## Profile Discovery
Nostria is built on the outbox model, which makes it possible to scale globally with ease. Everyone can post on their own relays, anywhere in the world, and still be discoverable by others. Unfortunately a lot of users on Nostr have been moving across multiple relays, often not bringing with them their profile information (and other events). With Nostria 2.0, we have implemented a robust profile discovery mechanism that will find a user profile, even when it's not available on the user's own relays - and then automatically publish their profiles to their relays, making them easily discoverable by others.

## Calendars
The world is big and it has many languages and cultures. To better support this diversity, we've added support for multiple calendar systems in Nostria 2.0. Users can now choose their preferred calendar system, such as Gregorian, Chronia (13 months) and Ethiopian. While the whole app is not translated to all supported languages yet, we did add Spanish and French just recently. There is so much new features added still, so when there is less new stuff, we will focus more on translations.
